Oraboni Foundation Leadership Uganda (OFLU)

ORABONI FOUNDATION has been established in 2022 to serve the community after the hardships created by the pandemic. It is a Community-based Organization with a focus on Health. ORABONI FOUNDATION understands Health based on the definition of World Health Organisation (WHO).This is a non-government organization.The main objectives and goals for this organization is to merge health related services access to vulnerable population especially single mothers and disadvantaged children as well as teaching them the word of God.
ORABONI FOUNDATION PROJECTS directly target the most needy and neglected groups such as PWDs, blind children, teenage children (10-14) and adolescent youth of (15- 22) years of age, orphans, widows, street children, older persons, women, PLHIV, Cancer patients, Key Populations-Sex workers and groups/movements /associations. The other beneficiary groups are schools, farmers and adult men.

  • Guiding Values:The guiding values and principles provide the moral and strategic basis for all the work of the Oraboni Foundation.
  • Dignity of the human person:The dignity of the human person especially single mothers is our foundational moral value. We reject the reification of these women, the poor and seek to make them not objects of our pity but subjects of their own development and agents of change. In this way, Oraboni foundation makes God's love for creation manifest in the world.
  • The health need:The Oraboni Foundation commits itself to combating extending health related services to people affected and promoting the rights improving their quality of life. We commit ourselves to restoring their sense of co-responsibility in building a better world through teaching them life skills and especially the word of God.
  • Unity goal:The Oraboni Foundation seeks to inculcate in its membership and its partnership with other non-governmental organisations and other global institutions a genuine sense of Unity, not as a feeling of sympathy but of empathy, of putting oneself in the shoes of the disadvantaged and seeing the world from their perspective.
  • Stewardship goal:The Oraboni Foundation commits itself to being in unity not only with people but with the whole of creation and therefore seeks to act in an environmentally sustainable way at all times.
