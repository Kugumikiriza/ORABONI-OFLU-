Dignity of the human person

The dignity of the human person especially single mothers is our foundational moral value.

The health need

The Oraboni Foundation commits itself to combating extending health related services to people affected and promoting the rights improving their quality of life.

Unity goal

The Oraboni Foundation seeks to inculcate in its membership and its partnership with other non-governmental organisations and other global institutions a genuine sense of Unity, not as a feeling of sympathy but of empathy, of putting oneself in the shoes of the disadvantaged and seeing the world from their perspective.

Stewardship goal

The Oraboni Foundation commits itself to being in unity not only with people but with the whole of creation and therefore seeks to act in an environmentally sustainable way at all times.

Guiding Values

The guiding values and principles provide the moral and strategic basis for all the work of the Oraboni Foundation.

AIMS

This is Oraboni Foundation Leadership Uganda (OFLU).

Merging health related services access to vulnerable population especially single mothers and disadvantaged children as well as teaching them the word of God.

  • Save the needy
  • Save single mothers

Aimed for young christians

To equip young Christians enabling them reach out with comfort from biblical scriptures in activities like healing crusades with doctors and nurses at the forefront, test and treat campaigns preaching the gospel.

To increase access to education, health care, decent work and sustainable living through income generating projects for everyone with a need.

  • Education
  • Health care

To address stigma and discrimination, prevention and response to sexual Gender Based Violence and improving Maternal and Child Health.
