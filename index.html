OBJECTIVES

Happy life Healthy life

To address stigma and discrimination, prevention and response to sexual Gender Based Violence and improving Maternal and Child Health.

To facilitate the process of growth, development care and love amongst the needy groups (single mothers, homeless children, orphaned children, youth and adolescent people) in the community.

To cooperate with government and non-government organizations with similar objectives locally and internationally.

To equip young Christians enabling them reach out with comfort from biblical scriptures in activities like healing crusades with doctors and nurses at the forefront, test and treat campaigns preaching the gospel.

To network and collaborate with key strategic parties, government, communities and individuals at national, regional and international level focusing on sexual and reproductive health and rights.

To engage in projects and fund mobilization efforts for the purpose of generating funds to run community activities in line with organization goals and objectives.

To increase access to education, health care, decent work and sustainable living through income generating projects for everyone with a need.

To promote biodiversity conservation through partnerships, participation and sustainable management of natural resources in the Teso Sub-region North-Eastern Uganda.

To lobby and solicit funds to support the organization through fundraising drives, donations and grants

To promote the human rights advocacy and in particular the rights of the children, young girls and marginalized groups.

To empower the single mothers with knowledge and life skills aimed at poverty eradication.

To work for the social and economic development of the underprivileged individuals, groups and communities through formation and management of Village Savings and Loans Association (VSLA), community based organization (CBOs)
